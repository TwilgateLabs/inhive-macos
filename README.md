InHive is a free, open-source VPN client. It connects to any provider that gives you a subscription URL — InHive does not sell subscriptions or run VPN servers.

## Supported protocols

VLESS (Reality / XHTTP / XTLS-Vision), VMess, Trojan, Shadowsocks, NaiveProxy, Hysteria2, TUIC, WireGuard, AmneziaWG, Mieru, DNSTT, SSH.

## Privacy

InHive does not collect telemetry. Your subscription URL, server pings, and traffic stats stay on your device.
